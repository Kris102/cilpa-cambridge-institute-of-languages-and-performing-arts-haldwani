01 — About CILPA

Start Your
Japanese
Journey.

CILPA's Japanese Language Foundation Batch is designed for complete beginners who want to learn Japanese seriously in a structured offline environment.

Students learn Hiragana, Katakana, vocabulary, grammar, conversation practice, pronunciation, and Japanese culture — all while preparing for the JLPT N5 examination.

With small batches, focused teaching, and direct mentor support, this course helps students build a strong foundation for higher Japanese levels, careers, study opportunities, anime understanding, travel, and communication.
